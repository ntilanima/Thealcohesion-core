# MTAAGRID_SYSTEM_MISSION
MtaaGrid is a PropTech-FinTech-GovTech hybrid designed to digitize the KES 630B annual rental flow in East Africa. It transforms fragmented cash flows into trusted Smart Housing Infrastructure.

## CORE_PROBLEM_STATEMENT
The rental market faces 30-40% arrears, low tax compliance (KRA eTIMS), and a lack of data visibility. MtaaGrid bridges the gap between Landlords, Tenants, and Government.

## KEY_INNOVATIONS
- VFS_TRUST_GRID: Unified framework for tenant/property management.
- KRA_ETIMS_BRIDGE: Automated tax compliance and withholding.
- IOT_SMART_LIVING: Integration of smart locks and utility meters.
- 3D_VISUALIZATION: AR/VR property tours to reduce tenant search friction.

## TARGET_METRICS_Y5
- MAU: 1,000,000+ Active Users.
- LISTINGS: 500,000+ Properties.
- GTV: KES 50,000,000,000+ processed transaction value.
